## Learnings
- NIST rules use flat structure with top-level `salience` for deny rules; constrain/escalate rules only have `salience_band` without numeric salience
- HIPAA rules use nested `rules:` lists with per-rule `salience` values
- role-restrictions.yaml has no `salience_band` field or top-level `action` — salience values must be validated against known non-overlapping bands
- All salience bands are non-overlapping: deny 170-190, scope_constraint 130-150, escalate 110-120
